# Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive Tableau Business Intelligence project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profitability trends, and operational KPIs using dynamic dashboards.

---

## Project Overview

This project consists of two fully interactive dashboards:

1. Sales Dashboard
2. Customer Dashboard

The dashboards help business stakeholders, sales managers, and executives analyze:

- Year-over-Year Sales Performance
- Profitability Trends
- Customer Behavior
- Product Performance
- Weekly Sales & Profit Trends
- Top Customers by Profit

---

## Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to:

- Track sales growth effectively
- Identify high-performing products
- Monitor profitability trends
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze customer loyalty and engagement

This Tableau solution provides a centralized analytics dashboard for business decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Dashboard

#### KPI Overview
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity
- Year-over-Year comparison

#### Sales Trends
- Monthly trend analysis
- Highest and lowest sales month identification

#### Product Performance
- Sales by sub-category
- Profit vs Loss comparison

#### Weekly Trends
- Weekly sales and profit trends
- Average weekly benchmark
- Above-average and below-average highlighting

---

### 2. Customer Dashboard

#### KPI Overview
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Sales per Customer

#### Customer Trends
- Monthly customer behavior analysis
- Highest and lowest performing months

#### Customer Distribution
- Distribution based on number of orders

#### Top Customers Analysis
- Top 10 customers by profit
- Sales, orders, and latest order tracking

---

## Interactive Features

- Dynamic Year Selection
- Cross-filtering between charts
- Region, State, City filters
- Product Category & Sub-category filters
- Navigation between dashboards
